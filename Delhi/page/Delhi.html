What to Consider Before Booking a Wedding Venue in Delhi

  • Date: Popular venues get booked up to months in advance, so confirm your wedding date as early as possible.
  • Guest count: Choose a hall, lawn, or farmhouse that comfortably fits your guest list.
  • Budget: Check both per-plate costs and rental charges before booking.
  • Location: Pick a venue with good connectivity to hotels, the airport, and transport hubs.
  • Parking: Ensure the venue has enough parking for your guests.
  • Closing times: Most Delhi venues have music cut-off and closing time restrictions. Confirm these in advance.

Frequently asked questions

What is the average cost of wedding venues in Delhi?

Most venues charge between ₹1500 and ₹3500 per plate. A one-day wedding in Delhi usually costs between ₹15-35 lakhs, depending on the venue, guest list, and decor.

Which are the most popular wedding venues in Delhi?

Top choices include The Leela Palace, ITC Maurya, The Imperial, Tivoli Gardens, and lavish farmhouses in Chhatarpur and NH8.

What is the best time of year to get married in Delhi?

The best months are October to March. The weather is perfectly pleasant, which makes outdoor weddings comfortable.

Do Delhi venues allow overnight weddings?

Most central venues follow music cut-off rules by 10-11 PM. Farmhouses and resorts on the outskirts may allow late-night or overnight weddings with prior approval.

Which localities near me in Delhi have the best wedding venues?

Popular wedding zones include Chhatarpur, Dwarka, Aerocity, South Delhi, and the NH8 stretch near Gurgaon.

How to find wedding venues near me in Delhi?

Use The Wedding Company’s platform to filter venues by budget, guest list, and location. Our planners arrange visits and handle the details so you can book with ease.
