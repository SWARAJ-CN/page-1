What to Consider Before Booking a Wedding Venue in Gurugram

  • Date: Popular venues in Gurugram get booked quickly, so block your dates early.
  • Guest count: Make sure the seating and floating capacities meet your needs.
  • Budget: Review rental charges, per-plate rates, and package inclusions.
  • Location: Choose a venue that is convenient from Delhi and Gurugram airports and highways.
  • Parking: Check parking availability for your guest count.
  • Timings: Confirm rules on music curfews, late-night events, and multi-day celebrations.

Frequently asked questions

What is the average cost of wedding venues in Gurugram?

The average cost of wedding venues in Gurugram ranges from ₹5-10 lakhs for a two-day wedding, depending on size, facilities, and services. Smaller banquet halls can host weddings under ₹5 lakhs, while luxury resorts often start from ₹8 lakhs and above.

Which are the most popular wedding venues in Gurugram?

Some of the well-known venues include The Leela Ambience Gurugram, Hyatt Regency Gurugram, ITC Grand Bharat, Trident Gurugram, Karma Lakelands, and The Westin Sohna Resort & Spa.

What is the best time of year to get married in Gurugram?

The most suitable season is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant for both indoor and outdoor weddings.

Do Gurugram venues allow overnight weddings?

Yes, most resorts and banquet halls in Gurugram allow overnight stays and multi-day celebrations. Music and outdoor event curfews may differ by venue, so it is best to confirm in advance.

Which localities near me in Gurugram have the best wedding venues?

Top areas for weddings in Gurugram include Sohna Road, MG Road, Golf Course Extension, Manesar, and Sector 29. These offer a mix of luxury resorts, banquet halls, and boutique venues.
