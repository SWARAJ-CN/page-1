What to Consider Before Booking a Wedding Venue in Udaipur

  • Date: Popular venues in Udaipur get booked months in advance, so plan early.
  • Guest count: Choose a venue that can comfortably host both seated and floating guests.
  • Budget: Review per-plate charges, rentals, and package minimums.
  • Location: Ensure easy access to the airport, hotels, and transport for guests.
  • Parking: Check if there is enough space for cars and buses.
  • Timings: Confirm music curfews, late-night rules, and permission for multi-day celebrations.

Frequently asked questions

What is the average cost of wedding venues in Udaipur?

The average cost of a wedding venue in Udaipur ranges from ₹30-70 lakhs for mid-range celebrations to ₹80 lakhs-₹2+ crores for luxury palace weddings, depending on guest count, decor, and venue type. Smaller off-season weddings can start around ₹25 lakhs.

Which are the most popular wedding venues in Udaipur?

Top choices include The Leela Palace, Taj Lake Palace, Oberoi Udaivilas, Jagmandir Island Palace, The Ananta, and Radisson Blu.

What is the best time of year to get married in Udaipur?

The ideal months are September to March. The weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor celebrations.

Do Udaipur venues allow overnight weddings?

City hotels often have music cut-offs around 10-11 PM. Resorts, palaces, and private properties on the outskirts may allow late-night or overnight functions with prior approval.

Which localities near me in Udaipur have the best wedding venues?

Popular locations include Lake Pichola, Fateh Sagar, Kodiyat Road, and the city palace belt.
