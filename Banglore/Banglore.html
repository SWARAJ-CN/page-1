Frequently asked questions

What is the average cost of wedding venues in Bangalore?

Most venues charge ₹1,200-₹2,500 per plate. A full one-day wedding usually ranges from ₹10-25 lakhs, depending on guest count, decor, and more.

Which are the most popular wedding venues in Bangalore?

Favourites include The Tamarind Tree, ITC Gardenia, The Leela Palace, Royalton Leisure, and resorts near Nandi Hills.

What is the best time of year to get married in Bangalore?

The best months are October to February. The weather is cooler, and outdoor venues are more comfortable.

Do Bangalore venues allow overnight weddings?

Most venues in the city allow overnight weddings for the couple, but do not allow music to play beyond 10 pm. Outskirts resorts may allow late-night or overnight weddings with prior approval.

Which localities near me in Bangalore have the best wedding venues?

Popular areas include Whitefield, Yelahanka, Kanakapura Road, Hebbal, and Vasanth Nagar.

How to find wedding venues in Bangalore near me?

Use The Wedding Company’s platform to filter venues by budget, guest list, and location, then visit shortlisted options.
